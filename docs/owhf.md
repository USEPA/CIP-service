## EPA Office of Water Hydrology Fabric

The [US Environmental Protection Agency](https://epa.gov) [Office of Water](https://www.epa.gov/aboutepa/about-office-water) provides via the CIP-service project several national hydrologic datasets for use in indexing, navigation and watershed delineation.  These datasets and logics allow for advanced analysis and discovery of water quality, remediation efforts and protection status.

CIP-service allows users to mix and match datasets to meet custom requirements.
